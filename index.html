Hi, I'm Esther!
Welcome to my page about my study abroad experience in Seoul, South Korea.

I created this page to encourage fellow college students to consider studying abroad for at least one semester! I attended Yonsei University during my junior year in Fall 2022. I had wonderful opportunities to learn more about Korean culture, to meet international students, and to explore beautiful locations unique to South Korea. This website will include recommended spots to see for some of the provinces that I visited during my stay!
